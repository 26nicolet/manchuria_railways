About the Data

Timetable Source

Train schedules are extracted from a 1940 Japanese railway timetable (滿洲支那汽車時間表) published in Kyūjitai characters. I'd like to thank my professor, Dr. Konrad Lawson, for providing me with the scans.

Railway Geometry

Railway lines are derived from the China Railways OSM and South Korea Railways OSM datasets, exported via the HOT Raw Data API on 2025-03-04.

  • manchuria_railways.geojson — Full Manchuria region rail network
  • kr_railways.geojson — Korean peninsula rail network
  • selected_railways.geojson — Manually curated subset (~96,000 features) used for route snapping

North Korean railways, largely absent from OSM, were reconstructed from historical maps as 45 synthetic line segments across 6 routes.

Station Coordinates

Station locations were geocoded via OSM Nominatim and manually verified/corrected against historical maps. ~600 stations are shown within the Manchuria bounding box.

Lodging & Activities

Hotel and entertainment data is extracted from advertisements and directories within the same 1940 timetable publication.
